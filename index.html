About

Bio:

Through immersing myself in the thriving Berlin Tech scene I'm transitioning from a profession based around managing and organising others, to a position where I'm engineering, building and creating myself.

My favourite things are Ultimate Frisbee, cooking & great food, travelling & exploring, skiing & hiking.


My Coding Story:

I felt accomplished and challenged as a Project Manager. I was managing other professionals', I understood the ins-and-outs of my projects, I was anticipating risks and solving problems. I was good at my job, but I wasn't satisfied.

I had a nagging desire to create, design and 'do' something myself. Coding and app development had always been on my radar but I never seriously considered a career change into Tech. In June 2016 I took the opportunity of representing Australia at the Ultimate Frisbee World Championships in London to take a career break and extensively travel Eastern Europe.

While travelling, I entertained my curiosity in website, application and program development. In September 2016, I settled in Berlin and committed to learn the art and science of Web Development.

Reach out on Twitter to follow my journey, lend some advice or join me in learning Web Development.
